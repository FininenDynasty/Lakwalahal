Our Programs & Services

The Lakwalahal Foundation provides trauma-informed spaces for healing, protection, and community rebuilding. Our services are rooted in compassion, legacy, and dignity.

Code Blue Shelters

Emergency overnight shelters activated during cold weather to ensure no person is left to face winter without protection.

Safe Havens

Transitional housing for families and individuals in crisis, offering a peaceful environment and critical support services.

Runaway Youth Outreach

Compassionate care for displaced and runaway youth: meals, hygiene supplies, shelter, and supportive counseling.

Legacy Protection Network

Programs to support Indigenous descendants, protect cultural heritage, and advocate for ancestral justice.

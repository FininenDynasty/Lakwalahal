Frequently Asked Questions

What does my donation support?

Your donation helps fund Code Blue Shelters, Safe Havens for families, outreach to runaway youth, and ancestral legacy preservation programs.

Is The Lakwalahal Foundation a nonprofit organization?

Yes, we are a mission-driven humanitarian and spiritual organization grounded in ancestral justice, protection, and community healing.

How can I volunteer or get involved?

You can visit our Contact page to express interest. Opportunities exist in outreach, shelter support, event organizing, and storytelling projects.

Do you provide housing to individuals or families?

Yes. Our Safe Havens and Code Blue Shelters serve individuals and families in immediate crisis, offering short-term and transitional housing solutions.

Do you work with Indigenous communities?

Yes. We honor and uplift Indigenous legacies through our Legacy Protection Network and community-rooted advocacy and outreach programs.

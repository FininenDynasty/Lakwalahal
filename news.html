April 2025 – Lakwalahal Launches First Safe Haven in New York

The Lakwalahal Foundation has officially opened its first transitional Safe Haven, offering warm beds, nourishing meals, and community support for displaced families. This marks a major step toward our mission to ensure no one is forgotten or left unprotected.

March 2025 – Outreach Program Helps 50 Runaway Youth

Our Runaway Youth Outreach program reached a major milestone by providing clothing, meals, and safe shelter to over 50 vulnerable young people during the first quarter of 2025.
